Falooda is a delightful and refreshing dessert popular in India.
Here's a simple recipe to make this delicious treat at home:


Falooda

Ingredients:
-1/4 cup falooda sev (vermicelli)
-2 cups milk
-2 tablespoons sugar
-2 tablespoons basil seeds (sabja seeds)
-1/4 cup rose syrup
-1/4 cup mixed nuts (almonds, pistachios, cashews)
-1/4 cup tutti frutti (candied fruits)
-2 scoops of vanilla ice cream
-1/4 cup jelly (optional)
-1/4 cup fresh fruits (optional)

Instructions:

Prepare the Falooda Sev:
-Cook the falooda sev according to the package instructions.
-Once cooked, drain and rinse with cold water.
Set aside.
Soak the Basil Seeds:
- Soak the basil seeds in water for
about 15-20 minutes until they swell up.
-Drain and set aside.
Sweeten the Milk:
-In a saucepan, heat the milk and add sugar.
-Stir until the sugar dissolves.
-Let it cool and then refrigerate.
Assemble the Falooda:
-In a tall glass, add a tablespoon of
rose syrup. Add a layer of soaked basil seeds.
-Add a layer of cooked falooda sev.
-Pour in the chilled sweetened milk.
-Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top.
-Garnish with mixed nuts, tutti frutti, and jelly or if using.
Serve:
- Serve immediately with a spoon and a straw.
-Enjoy your homemade falooda!
- It's perfect for a hot day or as a special treat.

About Us

Established in 2008 as Grower's Cup, our company is dedicated to fostering genuine connections with farmers.Our mission is to empower coffee enthusiasts worldwide by providing access to 25 premium specialty coffees.With our innovative Coffeebrewer, customers can enjoy freshly ground and brewed coffee anywhere, anytime.
We're passionate about delivering exceptional coffee experiences, allowing our customers to be their own baristas. We have always selected and sourced some of the best quality specialty coffee beans from independent small farm-hokders from all over the globe, hand-roasting and slow-crafting them in small batches in our artisan micro-roastery in Middelfart, Denmark.
This allowed us to refine each distinct flavor and provide a truly excellent cup of coffee to our customers.
